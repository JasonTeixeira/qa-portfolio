## 2. What Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to operate the site and respond to inquiries. Here's exactly what that is:

### 2.1 Contact Form Submissions

When you fill out the contact form on sageideas.dev, we collect:

- **Full name**
- **Email address**
- **Company name** (optional)
- **Inquiry type** (e.g., project inquiry, general question)
- **Message content**
- **Submission timestamp**

**Why:** To respond to your message and understand your needs. We do not add you to any mailing list without separate consent.

### 2.2 Newsletter Opt-In

If you subscribe to our email newsletter, we collect:

- **Email address**
- **Opt-in IP address** (to record consent)
- **Opt-in timestamp**

**Why:** To send you studio updates, project announcements, and occasional essays. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.

### 2.3 Meeting and Booking Metadata (Cal.com)

If you schedule a call through our Cal.com booking link, Cal.com collects:

- **Your name and email address**
- **Preferred meeting time and timezone**
- **Any notes you add to the booking**

This data is collected and stored by **Cal.com, Inc.** under their own Privacy Policy. We receive a copy of the booking details to prepare for the meeting. We do not store calendar data in our own databases beyond email notifications.

### 2.4 Payment Data (Stripe)

Any payment processing on sageideas.dev is handled directly by **Stripe, Inc.** We do not collect, store, or process your payment card information. When you pay via Stripe, you are subject to [Stripe's Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy). We may receive a transaction ID, payment status, and the last four digits of a card for record-keeping purposes.

### 2.5 Website Analytics (Vercel Analytics)

We use **Vercel Analytics** to understand how visitors use our site. Vercel Analytics is a privacy-friendly analytics tool that:

- Does **not** use cookies
- Does **not** store personally identifiable information
- Does **not** track users across sites
- Collects only aggregate, anonymous data such as page view counts, referring URLs, browser type, and country-level geography

No personal information is processed by our analytics setup.

### 2.6 Server and Infrastructure Logs (AWS CloudWatch)

Our application infrastructure runs on **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**. AWS CloudWatch automatically logs:

- Request timestamps
- IP addresses (anonymized where possible)
- HTTP method, path, and status codes
- User agent strings

These logs are used for security monitoring, debugging, and uptime management. Logs are retained for **30 days** and then automatically purged. We do not use server logs for marketing purposes.

### 2.7 Cookies and Local Storage

We use a minimal set of cookies strictly necessary for site function (e.g., session state, dark/light mode preference). We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. For full details, see our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookies).

## 4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

- **Contractual necessity** — Processing required to respond to a service inquiry or fulfill an agreement (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).
- **Legitimate interests** — Analytics and server logging to maintain a secure, functioning website, where those interests are not overridden by your privacy rights (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).
- **Consent** — Newsletter subscription and any optional cookies, which you may withdraw at any time (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)).
- **Legal obligation** — Where we must retain records to comply with US law (GDPR Art. 6(1)(c)).

## 6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

- **Contact form submissions:** 2 years from submission, or until no longer needed for business correspondence.
- **Newsletter subscriber records:** Until you unsubscribe, plus 30 days for processing.
- **Cal.com booking records:** Retained by Cal.com per their policy; we delete meeting notes within 1 year of the meeting date.
- **AWS CloudWatch logs:** 30 days, automatically deleted.
- **Payment records:** 7 years, as required by US tax law.

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights:

### 8.1 For Everyone

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Ask us to correct inaccurate information.
- **Deletion:** Ask us to delete your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
- **Opt-out:** Unsubscribe from email communications at any time.

### 8.2 GDPR Rights (EEA / UK Residents)

In addition to the above:

- **Restriction:** Request that we restrict processing of your data.
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Withdraw consent:** Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.
- **Lodge a complaint:** File a complaint with your local supervisory authority (e.g., the ICO in the UK, or the relevant EU DPA in your country).

### 8.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

- **Right to Know:** Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information collected about you.
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of personal information, subject to exceptions.
- **Right to Correct:** Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
- **Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing:** We do **not** sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

**California-specific disclosures:** In the past 12 months, we have not sold personal information to any third party. We have shared contact information with service providers (Resend, Supabase) solely for operational purposes.

### 8.4 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of the above rights, email us at **sage@sageideas.dev** with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will respond within **30 days** (or within the timeframe required by applicable law). We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.
